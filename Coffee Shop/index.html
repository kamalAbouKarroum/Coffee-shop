About Us

In the heart of downtown, nestled amidst the bustling streets and historic architecture, lies Barista. Established in 1995 by a passionate coffee enthusiast named Kamal ABou Karroum, Barista began as a modest venture with a simple goal: to provide the community with exceptional coffee and a cozy gathering place. With dedication and perseverance, Kamal transformed an old, abandoned building into a warm and inviting café, adorned with rustic wooden tables, soft ambient lighting, and the irresistible aroma of freshly brewed coffee. Over the years, Barista became a beloved fixture in the neighborhood, attracting locals and visitors alike with its friendly atmosphere and artisanal brews. Today, as it approaches its 30th anniversary, Barista stands as a testament to Kamal's vision and commitment to quality, serving as not just a coffee shop, but a cherished hub of community life and connection.
